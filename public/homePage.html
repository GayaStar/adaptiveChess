Key Features

  • Adaptive AI Opponent: Play against a Reinforcement Learning agent until you reach a 1200 rating; after that, face Stockfish for the ultimate chess challenge.
  • Live Rating System: Your rating increases or decreases after every game, ensuring a new level of competition each time.
  • Voice Control: Move pieces hands-free by speaking moves like e4 or Nf3.
  • Post-game Analysis: Instantly receive the top 3 best moves for every position after completing your game.
  • Learn Chess: Discover how each chess piece moves and how to write and read chess notation—perfect for new players.
